36-month cash flow trajectory

Cumulative revenue catches cumulative cost between months 14 and 18, after the up-front $100K and the slow first-year ramp. Customer count on the right axis.

Strategic read

Vertical SaaS with compliance moat. Not quick break-even, not frontier-model subsidy. $100K up-front clears the 12-to-18-month valley. Compliance investment is the defensible moat that justifies $399 per user pricing vs. $89 Spellbook tier.
